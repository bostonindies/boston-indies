Boston Indies is a community of independent game developers in Massachusetts supporting hobbyists, professionals, artists, academics, and anyone else who's excited about making games.

Our goals are to:

  • Foster a supportive community for local independent game makers.
  • Encourage creating games independent of large budgets and major corporations.
  • Serve as a brain trust for local independent developers, and create a positive environment for learning and getting critical feedback.

We welcome folks regardless of development platform, engine, or genre.

As long as you show up with an open mind, helpful attitude, and you're making your own games, you're welcome to join us!
